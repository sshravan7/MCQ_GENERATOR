## MCQ 1
Question: According to the survey conducted at Arizona State University, what percentage of students delayed their graduation due to the COVID-19 pandemic?
A) 40%
B) 13%
C) 29%
D) 11%
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What was the average decrease in weekly study hours reported by students due to COVID-19?
A) 5 hours
B) 0.9 hours
C) 4 hours
D) 2 hours
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: Which socioeconomic group was found to be disproportionately affected by COVID-19 in terms of delaying graduation?
A) Higher-income students
B) White students
C) Male students
D) Lower-income students
Correct Answer: D
